# Kozu Heavy Industries Parts Material Set

🌍 Languages: [日本語](README.md) | [English](README.en.md) | [中文](README.zh.md) | [한국어](README.kr.md)

## Overview
This is a parts set compiling the various part materials I have created so far.

## Contents
### Driver's Cab
* **EV-E301 series type**
* **E129 series type (or something like it)**: Made 7 years ago, so the quality is low.

### Roof Equipment
#### Air Conditioners
* **AU720**: Centralized air conditioner installed on the 209 series, etc.
* **AU736**: Centralized air conditioner installed on the EV-E301 series.
* **CU708**: Centralized air conditioner installed on the Tokyu 5000 series, etc.
* **HRB352-2**: Decentralized air conditioner installed on the Hankyu 9000 series.

#### Pantographs
* **PS38**: Pantograph (DC type) installed on the EV-E301 series.
* **PS37A + AC/DC equipment**: Pantograph installed on AC/DC trains like the E531 series.
                    <br>As a bonus, the messy piping unique to AC/DC trains is also included.
#### Other
* **Radio antenna**: The small beige box often seen on JR East trains.

### Underfloor Equipment
#### Underfloor Equipment Main Body
* **209 series type**: Modeled before the control equipment renewal.
* **Keisei 3700 series type**: Made 8 years ago, so the equipment layout and overall quality are terrible.
* **Tokyu 5000 series type**: Equipment layout is slightly different. I plan to remake it eventually.
* **Hankyu 1000 series type (or something like it)**: A byproduct of something I made for a certain project.
                                  <br>There is also an All-SiC type casing for the VVVF.

#### Bogies
* **DT61**: Axle-beam bolsterless bogie commonly seen from the 209 to E231 series generations.
* **DT79**: Axle-beam bolsterless bogie commonly seen on JR East trains from the E233 series onwards.
* **FS-365**: Minden-Deutz type bogie used on early Tobu 8000 series cars, etc.
* **FS-396**: S-Minden type bogie used on late Tobu 8000 series cars, etc.
* **FS-579**: Monolink bogie with bolster used on the Hankyu 1000 series, etc.
          <br>Light gray textures are also available!
* **ND-718**: Conical laminated rubber type bolsterless bogie.
* **TS-807B**: Axle-spring direct mount bogie used on Tokyu corrugated cars.
* **TS-1019**: Axle-beam bolsterless bogie used on the Tokyu 5000 series.

#### Couplers
* **Kozu-style tight-lock coupler**: Modeled likely after a JR East train. Can be equipped with 1-stage/2-stage electrical couplers.
* **KE97**: Jumper plug.
